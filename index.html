About Us

Welcome to TK Kidz International Pre School

Our mission is to create a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment where children develop a love for learning.

At TK Kidz Academy, we focus on the holistic development of every child. Our vision is to empower children with confidence, strong communication skills, and social awareness required for their future educational journey.

  • Friendly and caring teachers
  • Safe and nurturing environment
  • Focus on communication & confidence
  • Social skills development

Our Programs

Learning Tailored for Every Age

Comprehensive early childhood education programs designed to foster active learning through play.

Playgroup

1.5 - 2.5 Years

Introduction to the school environment, focusing on interaction, sensory activities, and basic motor skills.

  • Social interaction
  • Sensory play
  • Language development

Nursery

2.5 - 3.5 Years

Developing independence and early literacy through structured play, art, and music.

  • Pre-writing skills
  • Number concepts
  • Creative expression

LKG

3.5 - 4.5 Years

Enhancing cognitive abilities, phonetic awareness, and logical thinking with hands-on activities.

  • Phonics & reading
  • Math concepts
  • Environmental awareness

UKG

4.5 - 5.5 Years

Preparing children for formal schooling through advanced language, math, and social studies.

  • Fluent reading
  • Advanced math
  • School readiness
